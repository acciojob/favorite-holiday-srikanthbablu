Kyoto


Kyoto is a historic city in Japan known for its classical Buddhist temples, gardens, imperial palaces, and traditional wooden houses. It is a destination rich in culture and beauty.

  1. Kinkaku-ji – The Golden Pavilion temple with a stunning reflection on the lake.
  2. Fushimi Inari Shrine – Famous for its thousands of red torii gates.
  3. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove – A peaceful forest walk among tall bamboo trees.
  4. Gion District – Home to Kyoto’s traditional geisha culture.
  5. Philosopher’s Path – A beautiful walking trail lined with cherry trees.
  • Kaiseki – A traditional multi-course Japanese meal.
  • Yudofu – A tofu dish popular near Zen temples.
  • Matcha Desserts – Sweets made with Kyoto’s famous green tea.
  • Saba Sushi – Kyoto-style mackerel sushi.
